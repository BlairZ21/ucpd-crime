Crime on Campus

By Sahil Chinoy and Thomas Kim | Staff

November 13, 2015

The Daily Californian analyzed five years of UCPD crime logs. Explore an interactive map of crime around campus, view crime by time of day, see historical trends or read more about how we analyzed the data.

By area

Although crime reports tended to be more frequent on weekday afternoons, they were largely incidents of property crime — theft and burglary, as opposed to violent crime such as assault, which was more sporadic.

By month

Crime reports have decreased since 2010, though they exhibit a strong seasonal variation. Property and quality-of-life crime tends to drop during the summer months, likely because there are fewer people on and around campus.
